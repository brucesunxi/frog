Privacy Policy

Frog Frenzy · Last updated: July 8, 2026

Overview

Frog Frenzy is a casual arcade game. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how players can contact us about privacy questions.

Information We Collect

  • Gameplay progress: score, stars, unlocked levels, item inventory, coin balance, and similar game state.
  • Player identifier: an app install ID, and when available in the Android release, a Google Play Games or platform player identifier in hashed form.
  • Purchase records: product ID, purchase token, order ID, purchase state, coins granted, and related transaction metadata for Google Play in-app purchases.
  • Technical data: app platform, app version, timestamps, and basic diagnostic information needed to operate the game and prevent abuse.

How We Use Information

  • Save and restore your wallet, items, and level progress.
  • Verify Google Play purchases before granting coins.
  • Prevent duplicate purchase credits, fraud, and abuse.
  • Improve game balance, reliability, and player experience.

In-App Purchases

The Android version uses Google Play Billing for coin packs. Payment is processed by Google Play. Frog Frenzy receives purchase verification data, such as product ID and purchase token, so the backend can verify the purchase and credit coins to your game wallet.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share limited data only with service providers required to operate the game, including hosting, database, and Google Play purchase verification services.

Data Storage and Security

Game progress, wallet data, purchase records, and item records may be stored on our backend database. We use reasonable technical measures to protect this data, but no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Frog Frenzy is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under the age required by applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will review the request.

Your Choices

You may request deletion of backend game data associated with your player identifier. Some purchase records may be retained when required for fraud prevention, accounting, legal, or platform compliance purposes.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact the developer through the contact details listed for Frog Frenzy on Google Play.
